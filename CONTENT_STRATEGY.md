## 1. CONTENT PILLAR STRATEGY

### Pillar 1: Product Education & Material Science

**Objective**: Establish technical authority and help buyers make informed decisions

**Sub-topics:**
1. **Material Types Deep Dives**
   - HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene): Properties, applications, cost analysis
   - LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene): Flexibility advantages, use cases
   - PP (Polypropylene): Heat resistance, food-grade applications
   - PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate): Clarity, recyclability, premium options
   - Barrier materials: Multi-layer technologies for product protection

2. **Application-Specific Guides**
   - Seedling bags: UV resistance, drainage, biodegradable options
   - Industrial containers: Chemical resistance, stacking strength
   - Food packaging: FDA compliance, food-safe certifications
   - Agricultural products: Weather durability, cost-effectiveness

3. **Benefits & Performance Metrics**
   - Durability testing results
   - Cost per unit vs. lifetime value
   - Environmental impact comparisons
   - ROI calculators for switching suppliers

**Content Formats:**
- Technical blog posts (1,500-2,000 words)
- Comparison charts and infographics
- Video demonstrations
- PDF material specification sheets

**Sample Headlines:**
- "HDPE vs LDPE: Which Plastic is Right for Your Agricultural Products?"
- "The Complete Guide to Food-Safe Plastic Packaging in Kenya"
- "5 Hidden Costs of Low-Quality Seedling Bags (And How to Avoid Them)"
- "Material Science 101: Understanding Plastic Grades for Industrial Use"
